Lab 13 - Loops

Challenge

The challenge of this lab is practice loops by creating a function that loops through a list of numbers and prints a message if the number is a multiple of 3, 5, or 7.

Problems

One of the problems we ran into was just making the print statements look nice. We wanted to kind of replicate how the example had columns of numbers instead of one big list so we had to figure out how to do that. In addition, we had to figure out how to include the "-" in the output because it looked really messy without one. Also, after doing the bonus, it was a little difficult to do the form for the factors since there were so many different values to look out for.
