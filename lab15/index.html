Lab 15 - Ajax

Challenge

The challenge of this lab was use AJAX to grab info from external sources and implement them into our page. In this case we decided to use the Pokemon API to make a "Choose Your Pokemon(Starter)" kind of game where it randomly assigns you a pokemon once you click the button. It will include the Pokemon Name, type, and image.

Problems

For this lab, we are playing with APIs to have a better understanding of them. By working with different APIs, we are able to learn how to use them based on the documentation they gave us and how to use them in our own projects.
